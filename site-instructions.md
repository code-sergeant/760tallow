* **Column A: The Other Guys (Trim Fat)**

* **The Smell:** Carries a distinct "beefy" odor that brands try to cover up with heavy essential oils.
* **The Feel:** Softer, greasy, and tends to sit on top of the skin without fully absorbing.
* **The Shelf Life:** Higher moisture content means it goes bad (rancid) much faster.


* **Column B: Seven Sixty (Suet)**
* **The Source:** 100% premium internal kidney fat.
* **The Smell:** Naturally neutral and completely odorless.
* **The Feel:** Firm in the jar, melts on contact, and mimics your skin's natural oils so it absorbs clean.
* **The Nutrients:** Naturally packed with fat-soluble vitamins A, D, E, and K to rebuild weather-beaten skin.
